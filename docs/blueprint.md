# **App Name**: FynWealth

## Core Features:

- Unified Bills Dashboard: Consolidate all recurring bills (utilities, EMIs, subscriptions, rent) into a single, organized view and provide timely due-date reminders.
- Categorized Expense Overview: View and manage all recorded expenses in a categorized format to understand spending patterns at a glance.
- Manual Expense Entry: Enable users to manually input and detail individual expenses, including amount, category, and date.
- AI-Powered Expense Capture: Utilize AI tools for efficient expense recording through bill/invoice scanning (OCR) or voice commands, extracting and structuring relevant details automatically.
- Budget Tracking & Monitoring: Set and monitor spending limits against actual expenditures across various categories, visualizing adherence to budget goals without income capture.
- AI-Powered Financial Insights: Provide smart suggestions using AI tools for upcoming heavy spending months, identification of repeating/unnecessary expenses, and optimization of bill prioritization and timing.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant purple (#6366f1) for a professional yet forward-thinking and sophisticated brand identity, representing intelligence and foresight in managing finances.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated purplish-gray (#ECEEF2) to provide a clean and calm canvas, allowing content and primary elements to stand out clearly.
- Accent color: A bright orange (#f59e0b) to draw attention to critical actions, notifications, and key insights, adding an energetic and dynamic touch.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward, and crisp feel in titles and key numbers. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high readability and neutrality in all descriptive and transactional content.
- Employ clean, minimalist line icons that are clearly identifiable and align with financial, organizational, and analytical themes to enhance user comprehension.
- Adopt a dashboard-centric layout with clearly delineated sections for bills, expenses, budgets, and insights, ensuring a responsive and intuitive user experience across devices.
- Incorporate subtle and meaningful animations for state changes, expense capture feedback, and navigation, improving user interaction and perceived responsiveness.
